About

Through my years of training and study I have learned of the bodies ability to adapt as well as develop issues with our neuromuscular system. Pain, reduced mobility, weakness slows us down and inhibits us, as athletes and as moving human beings in general. I've seen first-hand the effictiveness of M.A.T. on myself and my clients and I believe passionately in restoring function and eliminating these problematic symptoms to allow us to find freedom in our movement. Feel free to contact me for more information or schedule an appointment, both are options here on this website. Thanks for stopping by.
